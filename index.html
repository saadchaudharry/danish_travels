    About Our Company

    We have been successfully serving our esteemed clients for over 20 years

    Since our inception, we have been at the forefront of connecting top talent with prestigious opportunities in the Gulf countries. With our head office strategically located in the bustling city of Mumbai, India, we have become a beacon of excellence in the recruitment industry.

    Our journey began in 1995 when we recognized a growing need for a professional and reliable recruitment partner for the Gulf region. Over the years, we have grown not only in experience but also in stature, building a strong network of clients and candidates that spans the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) nations.
